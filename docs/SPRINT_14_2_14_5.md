# Sprint 14.2–14.5

This increment completes the paper-account operations loop.

## Portfolio reconciliation

`PortfolioReconciler` compares broker and local positions and open orders. It reports missing positions, quantity mismatches, average-cost mismatches, and orphaned local orders with INFO/WARNING/ERROR severity.

## Order lifecycle

`OrderLifecycleEngine` applies idempotent broker events to a validated state machine and maintains an immutable audit trail with weighted fill-price aggregation.

## Live risk controls

`LiveRiskController` blocks operation when daily loss, drawdown, gross exposure, symbol concentration, stale data, unhealthy broker connectivity, or duplicate-order checks fail.

## Streamlit operations

`apps/terminal/pages/Execution_Operations.py` provides account, reconciliation, lifecycle, and risk views with a persistent paper-only safety banner and emergency pause control.
