# Sprint 10 — Quant Intelligence

Sprint 10 turns the Research Lab into a decision-support workspace.

## Capabilities

- Weighted multi-factor ranking using standardized ROC, RVOL, VWAP distance, gap, and sector strength.
- Strategy deployment scorecards with explicit paper-readiness gates.
- Strategy comparison tables.
- Optimization robustness scoring.
- Parameter heatmaps for stop-loss and take-profit stability.
- Walk-forward out-of-sample charts.
- Bootstrap Monte Carlo final-equity distributions.

## Safety boundary

A `PAPER_READY` scorecard is evidence for further paper testing, not permission for unattended live execution. Live-account routing remains outside this release.
